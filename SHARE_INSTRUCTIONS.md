### Option 2: Share the QR Code

1. **Visit the share page:**
   - Go to: `https://frnds.x/share`
   - Or click "Share App" button on the landing page

2. **Download the QR code:**
   - Click "Download" button
   - Save the image to your phone/computer

3. **Share the QR code:**
   - **Instagram Stories**: Upload QR code image and add "Swipe Up" link
   - **Campus Posters**: Print and hang around university
   - **WhatsApp Status**: Share as status update
   - **Email Signature**: Add QR code to email footer
   - **University Events**: Display on screens/projectors

## 🚀 Update Your Link After Deployment

After you deploy to Vercel/Netlify:

1. Get your production URL (e.g., `https://frnds-x.vercel.app`)

2. Update the link in `src/app/pages/SharePage.tsx`:
   ```typescript
   const shareableLink = "frnds-x.vercel.app"; // Your actual domain
   ```

3. Optional: Buy custom domain `frnds.x` and point it to your deployment

4. Commit and redeploy!
